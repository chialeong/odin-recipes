Burger

Description

A classic homemade beef burger that's juicy, flavorful, and easy to prepare. This recipe is perfect for grilling or pan-frying, and you can add your favorite toppings to make it uniquely yours. Enjoy a delicious burger anytime with this simple recipe!

With just a few basic ingredients, you can create a burger that's better than anything you'd get at a fast-food restaurant.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Form the ground beef into patties, seasoning each side with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the patties over medium-high heat until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  3. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bun and adding lettuce, tomato, cheese, and other desired toppings.
